### Question 1
Describe the situation in which you would use a digital version of Blokus.

**Answer**
I’d use a digital version of Blokus during a game night when my family and friends are gathered, but we’re short on time. For example, after a long week, we might only have an hour or two before bedtime, and I want to play something that’s engaging but doesn’t take forever to set up. The digital version would let us jump straight into the game without fumbling with physical pieces or explaining the rules again—since my kids and I all have different skill levels, the quick setup and simple rules make it fair for everyone. The strategy aspect keeps it interesting, but the short session means we don’t get bogged down. Plus, if we’re playing online with friends who can’t be there in person, the digital version handles that seamlessly. I’d also love to see different modes or challenges to keep it fresh, so we don’t get tired of the same layout after a few games.
