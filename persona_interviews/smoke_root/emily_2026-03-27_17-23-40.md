## Transcript

### Question 1
Describe the situation in which you would use a digital version of Blokus.

**Answer**
I’m thinking about a typical Friday night. My partner and I often have friends or family over after work, but by the time everyone arrives, we’re all exhausted and don't have the energy to spend forty-five minutes setting up a physical board game. We want to just turn on the system and start playing immediately. Since I have a niece and nephew who visit sometimes, I need something that doesn't require a complicated manual to explain, but still offers enough depth that my friends and I can actually strategize. The digital version would be perfect for this because I hate the cleanup—sorting those little plastic pieces and storing the board takes so much time that I lose the desire to play. I just want the strategy and the game itself, not the logistics.
